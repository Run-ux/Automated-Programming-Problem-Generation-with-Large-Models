A. Restoring Numbers

Pavel had two positive integers a and b. He found their sum s and greatest common divisor g, and forgot a and b after that. Help him to restore the original numbers.

Input
A single line contains two integers s and g (1 ≤ s ≤ 10 9, 1 ≤ g ≤ 10 9) — sum and greatest common divisor of the numbers a and b.

Output
If Pavel made a mistake and there are no such numbers a and b, output a single number - 1. Otherwise, output two positive integers a and b on a single line, separated by a space. If there are multiple possible solutions, output any of them.

Constraints
time limit per test 2 seconds
memory limit per test 256 megabytes
